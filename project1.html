News Package -- Rod Blagojevich's Pardoning

About this Project

This news package covers the February 10 pardoning of former Illinois Governor Rod Blagojevich. President Donald Trump commuted Blagojevich's 14-year-sentence back in 2020, but now gives an unconditional and full pardoning. Featured are some Chicagoans reactions.

My methodology for this package was conducting an interview with John Shaw, a political expert, to provide insight into the topic. Additionally, I gathered data from City Hall partisans to understand their perspectives on the pardoning of Rod Blagojevich.
